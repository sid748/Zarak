Our Story

Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Agra, Zarak United Private Limited is a new-age real estate company built on the belief that every piece of land holds untapped potential. Guided by a vision to create spaces that combine thoughtful design, functionality, and enduring value, we are shaping developments that redefine urban living and community growth across North India.

At ZarakUnited, we go beyond building structures — we transform landscapes into lasting opportunities.
